# Breadsaver

Take control of your money with zero-based budgeting the simple way.


## About

BreadSaver is a zero-based budgeting app designed to help you tell your money where to go — before the month begins. Whether you're paying off debt, building an emergency fund, or saving for something big, BreadSaver gives you the clarity and confidence to make it happen.
